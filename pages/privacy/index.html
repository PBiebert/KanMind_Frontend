Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how your personal data is collected, used, and protected when you use this application.

Data Collection

When you register for an account, the following data is collected and stored in our database:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your password (stored in encrypted form)

This data is required to create and manage your user account.

Use of Data

Your personal data will only be used to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Enable login and authentication
  • Assign tasks and collaborate within the application

Your data will not be shared with third parties or used for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in a database operated by the application owner. This is a demonstration project — data may be deleted at any time without prior notice.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
